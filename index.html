Introduction


I have made a chair for physically or paralyzed people who don't have leg, hand, or paralyzed, they cannot move anywhere independently. So they can control this chair with their mobile or tilting their head


Features


  • They can control it by pressing numbers on their mobile phone.
  • They can control it by tilting their head.
  • They can control it from anywhere.



How to use


There are three ways to control the chair.
  1. First of all, they need to switch on the chair and the cap, and then they have to wear the cap, when they tilt their head, the chair started moving.
  2. First of all, they need to connect their mobile phone with the chair through the aux cable and then call to the connected phone from the other phone, then they need to press the defined numbers to operate the chair.
  3. First of all, they need to turn on the Bluetooth of their phone and open the application called "RemoteXY", then they have to connect their phone with the chair, then they have to wear the phone holding cap, and they are able to control the chair by tilting their head.
